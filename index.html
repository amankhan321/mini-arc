The things your wallet can't do. Send a request link and get paid with one tap. Or drop in a list and pay everyone in a single signature — settled through Arc's sender-preserving batch, straight from your balance.

Request a payment

Create a link or QR that pre-fills the payer's screen with your address, amount, and note. They tap once — no back-and-forth.

02

Pay everyone at once

Paste a list of addresses and amounts and send to all of them in one signed transaction, using Arc's Multicall3From — no per-recipient approvals.

03

Receipts you can check

Every send returns the on-chain hash, block, and timestamp — one click confirms it independently on Arcscan.
