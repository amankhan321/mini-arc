A non-custodial checkout for Arc, the stablecoin-native L1 where USDC is the gas. Enter an amount, send it, and walk away with a receipt you can verify on-chain. No account, no middleman, no backend.

01

Non-custodial by default

Your wallet signs every transfer. Funds move straight to the recipient — they never sit on a server or pass through a third party.

02

USDC pays its own way

On Arc, USDC is the native gas token. There's no separate coin to acquire or top up just to move your money.

03

A receipt you can check

Every payment prints the transaction hash, block, and timestamp — one click confirms it independently on Arcscan.
